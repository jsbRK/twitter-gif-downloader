Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download a GIF from Twitter?
Simply copy the URL of the tweet containing the GIF, paste it into the input field above, and click "Fetch GIF". The tool will extract the media and provide download options in GIF, MP4, and WebM formats.
Does this work with X.com URLs?
Yes! This tool supports both twitter.com and x.com URLs. Simply paste any valid tweet link and the downloader will handle it automatically.
Is this tool free to use?
Absolutely! The Twitter GIF Downloader is completely free with no hidden charges, no registration required, and unlimited downloads.
What formats can I download?
You can download in three formats: GIF (animated image), MP4 (video), and WebM (web-optimized video). Choose the format that best suits your needs.
Why does Twitter store GIFs as MP4 videos?
Twitter automatically converts uploaded GIFs to MP4 video format for better compression and faster loading. Our tool converts them back to animated GIF format, so you get the original-style animation.
